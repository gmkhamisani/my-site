Welcome! This is the start of something new.

## Why I'm Writing Here

I created this site to have a place to share my thoughts without the noise of social media. Here, I can write at length about things that matter to me — whether that's technology, ideas, or just observations about the world.

## What to Expect

Here's what I'll be writing about:

- **Blog posts** — Technical stuff, tutorials, and thoughts on code
- **Essays** — Longer pieces where I explore ideas in depth
- **Short posts** — Quick thoughts and observations
- **Op-Eds** — My takes on current events and trends

## Stay Connected

If you want to follow along, the best way is to [subscribe to the newsletter](/newsletter). I'll let you know when I publish something new.

Thanks for reading.
